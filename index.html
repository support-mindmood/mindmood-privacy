Privacy Policy – MindMood

Effective date: September 7, 2025

Contact: support@mindmood.app

Who we are

MindMood helps you track mood and journal entries, and optionally generate AI insights. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

What data we process

On device (default): Journal entries, moods, sleep/smoking/alcohol fields are stored locally on your device. PDFs you export are saved to your device storage.

When you use Pro (online AI): For each AI request, the related text is sent securely to our server to generate the insight. Our server forwards only what’s needed to our AI provider (OpenAI) as a processor.

We do not sell your data. We do not use advertising SDKs.

How we use data

  • Provide core features (journal, calendar, exports)
  • Generate AI insights on request (Pro) and enforce weekly caps
  • Reliability and security (basic server logs)

Data sharing

  • AI provider (OpenAI): receives only the prompt text when you request an online insight
  • Payments: Google Play processes purchases; we receive purchase tokens to verify entitlements

Storage & retention

Local data stays on your device until you delete it (delete entries/history, factory reset, or uninstall).

Server-side (Pro only): minimal request logs may be retained briefly (e.g., ≤30 days) for abuse prevention and support, then deleted.

Security

HTTPS in transit; access controls on our backend.

Your choices

  • Use offline-only insights (Settings → “Always use offline insights”)
  • Delete data (delete entries/history or “Factory reset” in Settings)
  • Uninstall the app any time

Children

MindMood is not directed to children under 13.

International transfers

Data may be processed where our providers operate.

Changes

We may update this policy. We’ll update the effective date and, for major changes, show a notice in-app.
